## Build With ALIS

ALIS is open for people who want to help build this project and for people who want to study or fork the code for their own work.

- Contribute to ALIS through issues, discussions, and pull requests.
- Build your own project from the ALIS code under **AGPL-3.0-only**.
- Share source changes when the AGPL requires it, including network/server use.
- Use ALIS art, audio, lore, and world content only under **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** unless the maintainers give written permission.
- Do not use the ALIS name or logo for your fork without following the trademark policy.
