ALIS

Learn. Survive. Connect.

Open-source post-apocalyptic survival game built on Unreal Engine 5. Real places as the world. Systems grounded in real-world logic. Open code, open architecture, and a pact for honest design.

Systems Trust Vision Status

Core Systems

Physiology

The body model tracks calories, hydration, sleep, and condition. Fatigue accumulates and affects performance. Injuries degrade what you can do over time. Each vital interacts with the others through threshold states.

Inventory

Grid-based spatial packing. Items have physical size, weight, and volume. Storage comes from equipment — a backpack gives you space, losing it takes it away. Baseline is two hands.

World

Reconstructed from real terrain data — real streets, real building footprints, real elevation. Each location carries its own history and material context.

Mind

An inner voice that surfaces thoughts based on your state — hunger, surroundings, context. Guidance through awareness, driven by what is happening to you and around you.
